Student Bio

Rebecca Kalhorn, Ph.D.

I am a detail-oriented problem-solver with 15 years in the education field, earning my PhD in mathematics, coding for an online instructional tool, and teaching mathematics and statistics at both the collegiate and high school levels, where I have been recognized for my work (Granted the Joseph B. Whitehead Educator of Distinction Award - 2018, peer recognized teaching award - 2018, and outstanding TA - 2009). As chair of Priory's mathematics department, I have also lead/initiated several projects including restructuring the mathematics curriculum, establishing an inter-high school mathematics department gathering and idea exchange, and co-leading Priory's 2018 reaccreditation. All this in addition to sharing my passion for the technical language of mathematics and statistics, critical thinking, and problem-solving with my students. www.lipsum.com
